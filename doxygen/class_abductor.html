Detailed Description

Abductor type alien.

Abductor type aliens move around in flocks in order to find astronauts. When they find an astronaut they will catch and abduct it, moving slowly towards the top of the screen. If they succeed in abducting an astronaut, the astronaut will become a mutant. Abductors fire single bullets at the player if close enough.
